Lethbridge

Come Visit Lethbridge!

About 120km from the United States border is a small city, famous for nothing in particular, which many choose to call home. From its airy Chinooks, University and College, the lazy Old Man river running through it, to the converted Watertower restaurant, Lethbridge Alberta is a popular place for tourists during the summer season. Surrounded by lush prairies, and just a few hours away from Calgary, Waterton Park, and the aforementioned border, Lethbridge is a great place to start out in life, or to settle down into retirement. If you like to live in relative quiet but still like occasional excitement, Lethbidge is the place for you! I’ve lived in the area for over 20 years and hope to convince you to come visit.
